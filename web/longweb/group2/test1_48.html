Phillips, Wheeler Win West KY Open Titles

Send Email
Advertisement
By West Kentucky Star Staff
Aug. 05, 2018 | LEDBETTER
By West Kentucky Star Staff Aug. 05, 2018 | 08:46 PM | LEDBETTER
Tyler Phillips won the professional division of this year's West Kentucky Open Sunday at Drake Creek Golf Club.

Phillips, of Clarksville, TN, followed up his 68 on Saturday with a final round 69 to win by three shots over defending champion Rick Cochran III, of Paducah (72-68). Seth Arthur, of Metropolis (70-73) and Greg Stewart, of Benton, IL (73-70) finished tied for third.

Gabe Wheeler, of Sikeston, MO (69-70) beat Paducah's Josh Rhodes (65-74) in a one-hole playoff to win the amateur division for the second year in a row. Drake Stepter, of Paducah (70-70) finished third.
