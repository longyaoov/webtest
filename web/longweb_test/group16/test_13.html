5 So Good reasons why Almond Milk is good for your health

Full fat, low fat, no fat, skimmed, soy, almond, rice, oat, coconut; the variety of milks that are available to us in startling.
Full fat, low fat, no fat, skimmed, soy, almond, rice, oat, coconut; the variety of milks that are available to us is startling. After all, milk has always been a great source of calcium and other nutrients and is also an essential for our daily cups of coffee or tea. However, there is one type of milk that has been growing increasingly popular in the recent times – almond.
Almond milk is an easily available option of milk which is full of nutrients and tastes absolutely delicious. With the increasing rage around veganism and rising dairy sensitivity, almond milk is being seen as a great alternative that helps our health as well.
There are various reasons why almond milk is a great alternative for dairy products, ranging from its low calorie value, and high nutrients formula to the sheer delight of a unique and delicious taste. While there are other substitutes of dairy free milk, almond milk is by far the most conveniently available and can even be made at home, provided we have the patience. For the quick moving souls, almond milk is easily available in various stores and also comes infused with different flavors, opening your choices. Whether it is adding some healthy flavor to your oats or simply making your daily coffee healthier, almond milk can do it all and much more. Here is why almond milk is #SoGoodin5ways:
Low Calories

The biggest negative in consumption of milk has to be the high-calorie content of the product. While a glass of full fat milk has 140-150 calories, unsweetened almond milk only contains about 30-50. In fact, some brands of packaged almond milk, like So Good Almond Fresh Natural Unsweetend, contain 40% fewer calories than skimmed milk. This is a key reason why almond milk instantly becomes the go-to choice for calorie-conscious people who want to enjoy their coffee and tea guilt-free.

High Nutrients


While home-made almond milk is a great source of nutrients, packaged almond milk such as So Good Almond Fresh comes with added nutrients like vitamin A, B2, B12, and D.Highsource of Vitamin D and Calcium in almond milk is also helpful for good bone health.
Lactose Free / Dairy Free


The worst thing about having allergies or high sensitivity is the constant struggle of finding safe food to eat. Almond milk comes as a savior for many who are lactose intolerant or sensitive to soy as it is completely lactose, dairy and soy free. In addition to this, particular brands of almond milk like the So Good Almond Fresh are also gluten free, which allows people with gluten allergies to keep their health on check, without any worry.
Cholesterol Free


Rising cholesterol levels is one of the prevalent health issues that most people have to cope with. So Good Almond Fresh Almond milk, with zero cholesterol, perfectly fits into the diet of those who are battling bad cholesterol. This simple #SoGoodSwitch from dairy milk to almond milk along with a healthy active lifestyle can help keep youmaintain a healthy heart..
Tastes Delicious


There is a notion that healthy food doesn’t always taste great. However, almond milk is the best example to break this myth. In addition to its health benefits, packaged almond milk, like the So Good Almond Fresh, also offers a variety of flavors that are infused during extraction. From the subtle and simple vanilla flavor to classic chocolate, the variety and options of almond milk makes it all the more exciting. Even the Natural unsweetened almond milk comes with a rich taste of nuts and is absolutely delightful.
All these factors clearly prove that almond milk is the #SoGoodDairyFree alternative to dairy milk. While we struggle to make various switches for our health, this is one switch which is easy, instant, and does not compromise on our taste. If you are confused about the best brand for almond milk, So Good Almond Fresh has something for everyone – from goodness to health. Now that you know what makes almond milk so good, what is stopping you from making the #SoGoodSwitch? Here’s where you should head if you are looking to make the big switch.
