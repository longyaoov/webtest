Sir: Mr Waugh goes, this time, too far by far.

OK, he has gone clean off the Prime Minister and that is his affair. But the last sentence of the article (in con- junction with the heading) is not just a further expression of his convoluted opinions. Bombs at party conferences are no joking matter and to make them one is an insult.

I believe that many (even detractors) will agree that the courage and exemplary behaviour of Mrs Thatcher and all con- cerned in opening the 1984 conference on time after that tragic night, will endure as one of the highest of many Conservative achievements. Snide suggestions of a 'be- nign' Semtex replay are totally outrageous.

Iris Jollye

lb Brock Street, Bath
