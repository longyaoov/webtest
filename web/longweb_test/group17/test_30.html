Democratic Lawmakers Rally To Stop Gun Violence

In the wake of the mass shooting in Orlando, Democratic congressmen rallied in Chicago as part of a national day of action to stop gun violence. Lawmakers like Representatives Jan Schakowsky, Danny Davis and Mike Quigley joined advocates calling for a vote on gun control measures. Last week, they were sitting on the House floor for more than 24 hours demanding the same. Chicago Congresswoman Robin Kelly says Republicans who support the effort must also speak up.

By  Susie An
