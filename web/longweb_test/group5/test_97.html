    Synopsis

    Nifty has to hold above 11,500 to extend its gains towards 11,650 and then 11,760 levels.

    By Chandan Taparia

    Nifty opened positive on Thursday and witnessed sustained buying int erest throughout the session to extend the gains towards the 11,580 level. It formed a bullish candle on the daily chart and formed higher highs and lows for the third session.

    The index managed to cross and close above its immediate hurdle at 11,550 as the March F&O contracts expired. It now has to hold above 11,500 to extend its gains towards 11,650 and then 11,760 levels, while on the downside, support is seen at 11,450 and then 11,380 levels.

    Nifty continued its positive momentum on the last day of the series and registered a decent series-to-series gain of 7.31 per cent. The index closed at 11,570 against the February series close of 10,792.

    It witnessed higher rollover compared with that in last two months at 60.26% (in Feb) and 61.84% (in Jan) with better roll cost, which suggests longs were rolled over to the next series. This momentum could extend Nifty gains to new high at 11,760 and even higher.
