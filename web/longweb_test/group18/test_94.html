Some S&WB customers having troubles with online payments

Published: Jan. 16, 2019 at 11:14 AM CST

NEW ORLEANS (WVUE) -Some Sewerage & Water Board customers are having troubles paying their bills online.

The S&WB confirmed the outage on their Twitter page Wednesday morning.

According to the tweet, the issue is in the process of being fixed.

Customers can make payments in the meantime by visiting swbno.org. Customers can click “one time payment” under the Customer Service tab.

We are hearing customers are unable to make online payments and we are in the process of fixing this issue. In the meantime, payments are able to be made by visiting https://t.co/2f0uYA0cR6 and click “One-Time Payment” under the Customer Service tab.

— SWB New Orleans (@SWBNewOrleans) January 16, 2019
