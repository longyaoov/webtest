English KS1: Lakshmi and the Clever Washerwoman

BBC Teach > School Radio > English > KS1: Lakshmi and the Clever Washerwoman

Lakshmi Pt1 - Lakshmi Pt2 - Tiddalik Pt1 - Tiddalik Pt2 - Anansi Pt1 - Anansi Pt2

The traditional Hindu tale of Lakshmi and the Clever Washerwoman is told in two episodes. There is a new Resource Pack to go with the animation with worksheets and learning activities - suitable for use at home and in school.

It is almost time for the festival of Diwali - when the goddess Lakshmi walks among the people. A humble washerwoman finds a precious necklace on the bank of the river, where a crow has dropped it. When she discovers the necklace has come from the palace she knows she must return it…but how can she turn her find to her advantage?
