The 2015 PRWeek Hall of Fame Dinner in 18 photos

See the festivities that went down at PRWeek's annual Hall of Fame dinner at the Grand Hyatt in New York City.

09 December 2015

Celebrating its third year, the PRWeek Hall of Fame welcomed six new members to its ranks during a night of festivities on Monday, December 7. Six masters of PR – Mike Fernandez, David Finn, Joele Frank, Ginger Hardage, Gary Sheffer, and the late Betsy Plank – were honored for the profound impact they have had on the industry.
