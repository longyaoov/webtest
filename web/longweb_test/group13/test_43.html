    The ATL rapper linked up with a Japanese production duo for a new project.

    Jacob Moore
    July 5, 2011
    Blank pixel used during image takedowns
    Complex Original

    Most people associate Atlanta rapper Donnis with his southern roots, but lately he's been focused on a more international sound. Donnis' appreciation for the Japanese music scene started when he was stationed there with the US Air Force, and now he's incorporating that influence into his own music.

    Onitsuka Tiger has partnered with Donnis for a collaboritive EP with three Japanese producers that operate under the name Nippon Sounds. The EP will feature original music and remixes and will be available for free. Check out "All I Need" below.
