    UPDATED: October 27, 2018 at 10:33 AM EDT

    On February 25, 2006, JOSEPH RICHARD BOYD, of Huntingtown, MD; beloved husband of the late Mary C. Boyd; devoted father of Mary Jo Boyd, William Boyd, Patricia Posedenti and the late Joseph Boyd, Jr.; adored grandfather of Bridget Boyd, Jim and Regina Posedenti, John and Jennifer Warren, Michael and Kathy Davis, and great-grandfather of eight. Friends may call at the Rausch Funeral Home, 4405 Broomes Island Road, Port Republic, MD on Wednesday from 2 to 4 and 6 to 8 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial will be offered on Thursday, March 2, 11 A.M. at St. John Vianney Catholic Church, 105 Vianney, Prince Frederick, MD. Interment private. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Macular Degeneration Foundation, Inc, P.O. Box 531313, Henderson, NV 89053.
