Geri Horner isn’t bothered if ‘firm but fair’ criticism doesn’t fly on All Together Now

Cydney Yeates
Published February 25, 2019 12:01am Updated February 25, 2019 10:23am
Link is copied
Share this with
Comments

Spice Girl Geri Horner doesn’t care whether her honesty doesn’t win over contestants’ affections on All Together Now.

The 46-year-old singer, who’s returning to the judging panel alongside 99 music experts and performers next month,

Speaking to Metro.co.uk at an exclusive preview for the BBC talent contest’s return, Geri discussed how she goes about critiquing hopefuls.

‘I do feel it’s about being honest, caring to be honest – that’s more loving than saying something nice just so they like me back,’ Geri began.

‘I think that I’m going to say something that might sound firm, but fair. That’s more important than whether someone likes me back. And they might think that I’m not such a big star after that.

‘I just think as a respect to the show and to all the other contestants, I don’t think it’s fair if I just say nice things just so people like me.’
