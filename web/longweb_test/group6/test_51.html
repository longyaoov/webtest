Three Oregon hotels make TripAdvisor's list of top 25 hotels in U.S.

by KATU News

Sun, January 27th 2019 at 5:46 PM
Stephanie Inn in Cannon Beach - Photo courtesy TripAdvisor

    TOPICS:

    Expedia GroupHotelOperating SystemsService IndustriesTravelTripAdvisor

PORTLAND, Ore.- TripAdvisor released its top 25 hotels in the U.S. Saturday and three Oregon hotels made the list.

The Oxford Hotel in Bend came in 11th. The Inn at the 5th in Eugene finished 14th and the Stephanie Inn at Cannon Beach made 19th on the list.

The Stephanie Inn was also ranked the 16th best romance hotel in the U.S.

The Best Western Premier Boulder Falls Inn in Lebanon was recognized as 20th in best service hotel in the U.S.

“The hallmarks of Travelers’ Choice Hotel winners are remarkable quality, service and value,” TripAdvisor said in a release.

TripAdvisor says they determined the award winners based on millions of reviews and opinions collected in a single year from TripAdvisor travelers worldwide. This is the 17th year the travel site has ranked the best hotels in the U.S.

TripAdvisor named the following as the top five hotels in the U.S.:

  1. French Quarter Inn – Charleston, South Carolina
  2. The Nantucket Hotel & Resort – Nantucket, Massachusetts
  3. The Spectator Hotel – Charleston, South Carolina
  4. The Inn at Lost Creek – Mountain Village, Colorado
  5. Hotel Jackson – Jackson, Wyoming
