Cape Cod Today

About Us

At Cape Cod Today our mission is simple. We want to make you love “the Cape” as much as we do! We do that by publishing simple, informative resources for anyone that is looking for information about Cape Cod. If you are a current resident, future resident or vacationer we want to share our love of Cape Cod with you.  

Meet The Team

Kathy Jameson

Managing Editor

Kathy’s love for the Cape Cod community began in childhood, as she made yearly vacations here with her family. On one of these family vacations she met her her now husband and they decided to make Cape Cod their home to raise her family. Her passion for the Cape and its people is evident in her writing for Cape Cod Today, where she shares her personal experiences and insight into the unique culture and lifestyle of this beautiful peninsula.

Kris Lippi

Real Estate Broker

Kris is a Massachusetts licensed real estate broker at Cape Cod Today, owner of the brokerage Get Listed Realty and the popular real estate website ISoldMyHouse.com. The Cape Cod real estate market is unique and requires deep local expertise to help home buyers and sellers. Kris has a network of the top real estate agents across all of Cape Cod that have deep knowledge of the neighborhoods they work in.
