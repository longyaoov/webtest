Britain's Princess Eugenie marries Jack Brooksbank: See photos from the wedding

  • Published: Oct. 12, 2018, 12:20 p.m.
By
  • cleveland.com

Fans lined the streets near Windsor Castle for a second time this year as Britain's Princess Eugenie celebrated her wedding to Jack Brooksbank.

The ceremony took place at St. George's Chapel, the same location as Prince Harry's wedding to Meghan Markle earlier this year.

Eugenie is the daughter of Prince Charles' younger brother, Andrew, and is currently 9th in line to the throne of England.

Much like the prior royal wedding, numerous celebrities were in attendance including Demi Moore, Naomi Campbell and Cara Delevingne.
