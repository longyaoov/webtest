    Soldiers help unload supplies at the Stephen Jackson Academy point of distribution. The supplies are for Port Arthur, Tx residents who were affected by Hurricane Harvey. Former NBA player Stephen Jackson organized for the academy to be stocked from donations from all over the country. The truck being unloaded was from California. US Army Video by SGT James Dansie.
