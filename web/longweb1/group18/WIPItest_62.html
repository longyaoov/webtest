Pennsylvania is planning an enterprise identity management system to increase the security of its networks and computer systems.

The RFP under development is part of the state's Operation Secure Enterprise, which will serve as the foundation for employees, citizens and businesses accessing state information on the Web. An RFP is expected in February 2005.
