This weekend marks the global release of the highly anticipated adidas Originals Yeezy Boost 350 V2 Cream White iteration. With many sneakerheads eyeing the release of this pair for quite some time now, XXL is here to let you know where to buy the acclaimed sneakers.

The upcoming Yeezy Boost 350 V2 is crafted with premium materials that infuse adidas’ technology with Kanye West’s touch. The innovative 350 V2 features an upper composed of PrimeKnit material in three impactful hues of yarn. Dressed entirely in a cream white colorway, the signature ‘SPLY-350″ stripe blends in with the shoe's white layers, while a semi-translucent rubber outsole and TPU sidewalls create a striking effect providing superior traction.
