UPDATE: Vehicle Involved in Rollover Stolen

Bill HendersonPublished: October 7, 2012
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

 ST. CLOUD - Saint Cloud Police arrested an 18 year old Waite Park man after he fled the scene of a one-car rollover Thursday night.

Police responded to the 1200 block of 6th Avenue South where the accident happened.

They located the driver Fuad Abdi Jama of Waite Park in the 1100 block of 9th Avenue shortly after a foot pursuit. Jama was booked at the Stearns County Jail and later released on charges of no valid drivers license, fleeing on foot, minor consumption and 4th degree DWI.

Police later learned from the owner of the car that it had been stolen from the east side of Saint Cloud and Jama was also charged with theft of a motor vehicle.
