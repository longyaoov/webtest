SMSU WRESTLING: UMary downs SMSU to conclude its dual season

Local Sports

Feb 11, 2019

BISMARCK, N.D. – The Southwest Minnesota State wrestling team concluded conference play with a 27-12 loss to UMary on Sunday afternoon.

SMSU (1-9, 1-8 NSIC) earned three bout victories while UMary (6-4, 4-4 NSIC) earned the other seven.

The dual began at 141 and it wasn’t until 157 when Cortez Arredondo maintained a perfect record in conference play with a decision victory over UMary’s Devin Steidler.

Kegen Fingalsen followed up Arredondo’s win with a win by fall at 4:05 to give SMSU a 9-6 lead. UMary then earned a major decision victory over Nick VonRuden to head into intermission with a 10-9 lead.

UMary’s Lance Jarrett returned from intermission to win by fall at 5:57 to extend the lead 16-9.

Griffin Osing gave SMSU its last attempt to cut the deficit with a decision victory over Ryan Stottler to come within four points at 16-12 before UMary would take the final three bouts for the victory.

Sunday’s dual was the final conference dual for SMSU seniors; Cortez Arredondo, Jordan Biehn, Kegen Fingalsen, Griffin Osing, Nick VonRuden, and Zach Beaumaster.

157: Cortez Arredondo wins by decision (6-1) over Devin Steidler. UMary 6, SMSU 3.

165: Kegen Fingalsen wins by fall at 4:05 over Austin Eichmann. UMary 6, SMSU 9.

Saturday

Minot State 29, SMSU 12

MINOT, N.D. – The Southwest Minnesota State wrestling team (1-6, 1-7 NSIC) fell to Minot State (5-9, 2-5 NSIC) 29-12 on Saturday afternoon.

SMSU won three bouts on the night with the first victory coming with Cortez Arredondo at 157, to extend his 7-0 undefeated conference record with a win by fall at 5:59 over Max Miller.

Dru Kuchenbaker and Griffin Osing each earned a decision victory for the remainder two victories in the dual. This afternoon’s victory for Osing was his second consecutive conference victory.
