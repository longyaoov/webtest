OPINION: Visual imagery has not lost its power

Wandering round hilltop towns in Umbria this summer gave me plenty of opportunity to study Italian painting from the 13th to 16th centuries. What struck me was not the quality of execution, nor the stylistic variation over time, but the sheer repetition of subject and the clear reinforcement of a set of messages.
