Government Fails to Debate 1997 and 1998 Budgets

Ljubljana, 17 July - The Government did not discuss draft budgets for 1997 and 1998 at today's session, Vice-Premier Marjan Podobnik told a news conference following the session on Thursday. He expressed hope that an agreement would be reached to limit public expenditure and keep the budget below SIT 730 billion (about US$ 4.65b).
