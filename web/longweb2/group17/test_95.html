Sanctuary on Camelback Mountain Resort & Spa Announces Artist in Residence Curator, Linda Kilgore

By: Linda Margaret Kilgore
 
 
Sanctuary's Artist in Residence Curator, Linda Kilgore
PARADISE VALLEY, Ariz. - Oct. 11, 2018 - PRLog -- Linda Margaret Kilgore has been selected as the curator of the Artist in Residence program this season at the beautiful Sanctuary on Camelback Mountain Resort & Spa. A well-known sculptor and painter, Kilgore has exhibited her work both nationally and internationally, is the recipient of numerous arts' awards, including a Rockefeller Grant, and her work has been featured in numerous publications both online and in print. Kilgore's educational background includes formal studies of psychology and philosophy, as well as several years of independent art studies abroad in Italy, France, England, and Mexico. In addition to being a working artist, she brings several years of gallery and museum experience to the Residency Program.

"We are excited and looking forward to the upcoming season this year, where we will have an interesting mix of wonderful, well-known and emerging Arizona based artists along with work in a variety of mediums and with different approaches to their work. We have a great season coming up—Sanctuary's beauty and elegance provide a wonderful setting for showing art," states Linda Margaret Kilgore.

The new 2018/2019 season will showcase Arizona based artists, kicking off this November 2018, concluding with its final residency in March 2019. Each residency is one-month duration, which includes an evening Opening Reception "Sunset Cocktails" for each artist during his or her residency where guests will have the opportunity to meet the artist while enjoying the spectacular sunset over light fare and wine (hosted on a Thursday night beginning of each month) and is free to the public. Each artist will be present and discuss his or her work. Each artist will host two workshops during their month long residency, to be held on Saturday mornings at the Sanctuary on Camelback Resort.

Sanctuary's "Artist in Residence" program provides the opportunity to meet and learn from spectacular artists across a wide variety of mediums in Sanctuary's inspirational setting. For information about the Artist in Residence program visit https://www.sanctuaryoncamelback.com/dining/special-events/artist-in-residence/.

For more information about the Artist in Residency Program at Sanctuary please contact Linda Margaret Kilgore at info@lindamargaretkilgore.com
