    TUESDAY

    Al-Anon

    A support group for families and friends of alcoholics. Women’s stag meeting at Little Company of Mary San Pedro Hospital, Van Camp Conference Room, 1300 W. Seventh St., San Pedro, 7 p.m. Tuesdays. Call (310) 544-9668.

    Breast Feeding

    La Leche League of Greater Torrance meets monthly to cover breast-feeding questions and concerns for expectant mothers and experienced nursing mothers. La Romeria Park, 19501 Inglewood Ave., Torrance at 10 a.m. on the last Tuesday of the month. Membership is $36, but is not required to attend meetings or get phone help. Call (310) 515-2002.

    Breast Cancer

    Refugio Spanish-speaking support group for breast cancer survivors, sponsored by the YWCA and the Susan G. Komen Foundation. Harbor-UCLA Medical Center, Trailer N-24, 1124 W. Carson St., Torrance, 9 a.m. to noon the second and fourth Tuesday of the month. Call (310) 547-0831.

    Walking Together support group for women who have had an abnormal mammogram and are waiting for test results (English and Spanish). Harbor-UCLA Medical Center, Trailer N-24, 1124 W. Carson St., Torrance, 1 to 3 p.m. the second and fourth Tuesday of the month. Call (310) 547-0831.

    Diabetes

    Informal diabetes networking group meets to learn the latest medical information about diabetes care from experts. Torrance Memorial Medical Center, 3330 Lomita Blvd., Health Conference Center Building on Medical Center Drive, one-story building at parking garage, west side of medical center (meeting rooms 1 or 4) from 7 to 9 p.m. the second and fourth Tuesday of the month. Call (310) 370-3228.

    Eating Disorders

    Overeaters Anonymous, a fellowship of individuals who through shared experience, strength and hope, are recovering from compulsive overeating, anorexia and bulimia. Journey of Faith Church, 1243 Artesia Blvd., Room C-1, Manhattan Beach, 7:30 p.m. Tuesdays. Call Jennifer at (310) 542-7921.

    Multiple Sclerosis

    Harbor/South Bay Multiple Sclerosis support group meeting. The Neighborhood Center, McMasters Park, 3612 Artesia Blvd., Torrance at 1p.m. the second and fourth Tuesdays of the month. Call (310) 769-0694.

    Cancer Support

    Lymphedema support group explores latest treatment techniques. Torrance Memorial Medical Center, Health Conference Center Conference Room, 3330 Lomita Blvd., Torrance, noon to 1:30 p.m. the last Tuesday of the month. Call Paula Bauer at (310) 517-4665 or Anne Clary at (310) 376-3550.

    Mental, Emotional

    Mental health workshop provided by Recovery, Inc., where participants learn life coping skills. First Christian Church, 2930 El Dorado Ave., Torrance, 2:30 p.m. Tuesdays. Meetings are free and all are welcome. Call (310) 322-6411.

    Panic

    Panic Assistance League support group. Beach Cities Health Center, 514 N. Prospect Ave., Redondo Beach at 7:30 p.m. Tuesdays. $5 donation. Call (310) 374-4298.

    Weight Control

    T.O.P.S. (Take Off Pounds Sensibly) Chapter 148. Harbor City Recreation Center, 24901 S. Frampton Ave., Harbor City, 9:30 to 10:30a.m. Tuesdays. Call (310) 325-1583.

    WEDNESDAY

    Healthy Living Workshop

    Anti-aging expert Dr. Howard Murad will be joined by other health experts for “Be Your Personal Best.” The interactive presentation will focus on whole body health and total wellness. Presenters will share their knowledge for living life to the fullest. Following the presentation, attendees will get to tour the Murad facility, meet the staff, and have their questions answered. The Murad Inclusive Health Center and Spa, 2141 Rosecrans Ave., Plaza Level, El Segundo, 6 to 8 p.m. Wednesday. RSVP by calling (310) 277-0437.

    Healthy Self-Care Series

    Unity South Bay continues its series with a program on “The Jeunesse System,” a noninvasive face-lift. 2545 W. 237th St., Torrance, 7p.m. Wednesday. Call (310) 326-2760.

    Weight Control

    T.O.P.S. (Take Off Pounds Sensibly) Cal 186. 560 N. Western Ave., San Pedro., 7 p.m. Wednesdays. Call Esther Nunez at (310) 547-2768.

    THURSDAY

    Al-Anon

    Al-Anon, a support group for families and friends of alcoholics. Methodist Church, 1551 El Prado, Torrance, 10 a.m. and 7:30 p.m. Thursdays; Alateen meets at the same time as the evening session. Call (310) 544-9668.

    Nar-Anon

    Support group for family members and friends of drug addicts. First Christian Church, 2930 El Dorado Ave., Torrance. 7:30 p.m. Thursdays. Call (310) 534-8188.

    Ostomy

    The South Bay Ostomy Association meeting will feature Avier Varges, a dietician who will give an updated view to ostomy patients on how to design meals to extend life while caring for their ostomies. Torrance Memorial Medical Center, West Tower, Room B, 3330 Lomita Blvd., Torrance, 7:30 p.m. the fourth Thursday of the month. Call (310) 374-6748.

    Weight Control

    T.O.P.S. (Take Off Pounds Sensibly), San Pedro. Peck Park, Clubroom A, 560 N. Western Ave., 10 to 11 a.m. Thursdays. Call (310) 547-1098.

    T.O.P.S. 2403, Carson. 23303 Caroldale Ave., Carson, 9:30 a.m. Thursdays. Call Elsie at (310) 830-9084.

    SATURDAY

    Yoga Meditation

    Brahma Kumaris South Bay Meditation Place March open house. Highlights include a brief introduction to the Brahma Kumaris and Raja Yoga meditation, group discussion on spirituality, and refreshments. 22940 Maple Ave., Unit 2, Torrance. Call (310) 891-3008 or e-mail maureen.halikis@us.bkwsu.org.

    Yoga

    Bonnie Wulff, a certified yoga instructor, will present a low-impact interactive program on gentle exercise for the older adult. Participants should bring a towel and a bottle of water. Chair only participants are welcome to attend. St. Paul’s Lutheran Church, 31290 Palos Verdes Drive West, Rancho Palos Verdes, 10 a.m. to noon, Saturday. RSVP to the church office at (310) 377-6806.
