Sony Xperia T, bond with O2 and Three UK

Categories O2 Sony Three
Author by James September 29, 2012
1,649 1

If you are over here in the United Kingdom and have a penchant for Sony Android smartphones, in particular the Sony Xperia T, and have been waiting for the smartphone to become available on O2 or Three, then you just might like to know that the Xperia T is now available to purchase on both O2 and Three UK.

According to an article over on Engadget, the Sony Xperia T is known as the ‘Bond phone’ due to Daniel Craig toting the Android Ice Cream Sandwich handset around in the latest Bond movie Skyfall, and apparently O2 is offering the Xperia T with custom Bond ringtones, and pre-release pictures, whilst Three is offering the Sony Xperia T with unlimited data.

For those that might be interested in snapping up the Sony flagship smartphone, the Sony Xperia T offers the user a 4.55-inch scratch resistant 1280 x 720 TFT touch screen along with a 1.5GHz Qualcomm Krait MSM8260A dual core processor, a 13 megapixel rear facing auto-focus camera with LED flash, a 1.3 megapixel front facing camera, 1GB RAM, 16GB internal memory, microSD expansion up to 32GB, NFC, Bluetooth, WiFi, aGPS, Sony Mobile Bravia Engine, measures 129.4 x 67.3 x 9.35mm with a weight of 139 grams and has a 1850mAh battery.

So if you really want to get a touch of 007 on your smartphone, and considering picking up the Sony Xperia T from either O2 or Three, price plans for the Android handset kick off at 30-quid per month, with Three also offering the device on their One Plan at 34-quid a month, whilst O2 also offer the Xperia T from free to Â£179 a month or on Pay as you Go for Â£429.99.

Any of our Android faithful readers here in the UK considering snapping up the Xperia T from either Three or O2?
