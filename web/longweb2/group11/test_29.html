British women trek 'from London to Sydney' - and its all down to their love for shopping

Last updated at 12:33 19 February 2008


Most women would probably balk at trekking from London to Sydney, but apparently, if shopping is involved, they are prepared reconsider.

A new poll reveals that the average British women will trek a staggering 10,820 miles during their lives - just by wandering around the shops.

This is the equivalent of plodding all the way from the English capital to Sydney, Australia.

Women normally embark on 98 trips to the High Street or local shopping centre during their lifetime.

At just under two miles a time, this adds up to 171 miles each year.

In comparison, men walk only 140 miles around the shops each year - or 8,151 miles during the average adult lifetime.

A spokesman for High Street retail chain Debenhams, which conducted the poll, said: "It's no secret that women like to shop but it's staggering how many miles they will cover in a lifetime.

"As shopping is such an enjoyable experience, I think women will be surprised how far they do travel.

"And as we are supposed to take 10,000 steps a day, shopping can prove a great way to achieve this."

The study also revealed that almost 40 per cent of women reckon they actually cover more distance during one shopping trip than they do the rest of the week put together.

The poll, of 2,000 women, also showed that some women expect to walk further than two miles a time when they are out enjoying retail therapy with friends.

More than half of the women polled said they considered a shopping trip as a day's exercise.

Almost 15 per cent said they justified an expensive trip to the shops by thinking about the calories they have burnt and the miles they have walked.

An unfortunate 35 per cent of women said gruelling shopping trips had ended up with them limping home with an injury.

But three quarters do go prepared though by wearing sensible shoes, knowing they are going to walk several miles around the shops.

A spokesman for Debenhams added: "It is amazing to see actually how much of a toll a shopping trip can take on a woman, no wonder we are often so tired after a day on the High Street.

"Debenhams offers a complete shopping experience under one roof. Women no longer need to walk for miles to find the perfect outfit.

"As well as over 22 Designers at Debenhams and a vast selection of own-brands, Debenhams also plays host to the top cosmetic houses ensuring a successful trip without the sore feet."
