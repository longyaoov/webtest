The success of the Jewish Studies program depends on the generous support of people who value scholarly education and research. These are the Friends of Jewish Studies, a group of UC Davis alumni and community members who make annual gifts in support of the program. Our Friends help the program by funding undergraduate awards, public lectures and readings, workshops and conferences--all the essentials that help create a more robust and dynamic academic environment for our faculty, students, and friends from the community.
