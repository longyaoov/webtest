New Music: Kat Dahlia - 'Crazy'

She showed us the true definition of a “Gangsta” on her breakthrough hit, and now Kat Dahlia heads into 2014 with her infectious new single “Crazy.” The 23-year-old Cuban-American singer-songwriter is overcome by a love so powerful it makes her forget the past.

“Tell me ’cause I don’t understand/ How someone can just walk into your life/ And everything before them you forget/ I’m crazy, crazy, crazy for you,” she sings on the Glass John production.

Her full-length debut My Garden is set for a summer 2014 release. She has been in the studio with Salaam Remi, Timbaland, Future, Missy Elliott, Rico Love, Stargate, Naughty Boy, Illangelo, and Fraser T. Smith.
