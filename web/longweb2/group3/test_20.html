WEBINAR: The Power of Podcasting


The Power of Podcasting

Tuesday, December 1, 2015
1:00 pm to 1:45 pm ET

Podcast advertisements are five times as effective as web ads. —Palisades Media Group

17% of people say they listen to at least one audio podcast a month, whether it’s in the car or on transit, cycling or in the gym. Podcasts can capture an audience of decision makers and influencers that is eager to consume content that is entertaining and engaging.

Join ITWC CIO Jim Love and James Roy, President of Digital Reno and former CBC Radio broadcaster, for an informative webinar on The Power of Podcasting.

We’ll look at how easily you can take advantage of this new media platform and how it can help you engage potential clients. We’ll also look at audio versus video podcasts, plus which might work best in your circumstances.

MODERATOR
Jim Love
CIO, ITWC

SPECIAL GUEST
James Roy
President, Digital Reno
