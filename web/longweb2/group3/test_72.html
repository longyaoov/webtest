Bates Hamburgers in Livonia celebrating anniversary with 60-cent burgers

Susan Selasky
Detroit Free Press

Bates Hamburgers in Livonia is celebrating its 60th anniversary in a big way.

From 7 a.m. to 11 p.m. Thursday, the restaurant will be offering hamburgers for 60 cents and cheeseburgers for 75 cents. There’s a limit of 24. Guests get a free soft drink with the purchase of six burgers. Phone orders will not be accepted during the special burger offer.

Also in celebration of the anniversary, Bates is offering a deal on shakes: They'll be 60 cents off throughout the month. That means a small shake costs $1.69 and a large is $2.39.

Bates, at 33406 Five Mile in Livonia, has been family-owned for 60 years and is currently owned by John and Barb Bates. A location in Farmington Hills has been operating for 40 years.
