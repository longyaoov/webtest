Blue ribbon winners

Staff Writer
Fosters Daily Democrat

ROCHESTER — Participants of The Homemakers Day Out Adult Day Care Program were awarded four blue ribbons for their entries in this year's Rochester Fair.

Displaying their entries and ribbons, from the left, are: Orman Dodge of Rochester for a shadow box in the wood craft category; Lois McClaran of Strafford, Rosemarie Blampye of Rochester and Sophie Willett of Somersworth in the quilting category.

Day Out participants were awarded a total of 22 ribbons at this year's event. Others ribbon winners were: Susan Smith, Gwen Morrow, Doris Yergeau, Rita Parker, Susan Wells, Madeline Thompson, Lola Chalaire for their quilts; Robert Hibbard, Paul Ferland, John Paul Forcier, Richard Roy, Roger Gosselin and Ray Chagnon in the wood crafts category; Rosemarie Blampye and Rita Parker in the afghan category and Susan Wells and Doris Yergeau in the latch work rug category.
