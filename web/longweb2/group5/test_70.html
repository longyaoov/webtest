SNB’s Jordan: Looking forward to others tightening monetary policy

NEWS | 09/14/2018 04:16:08 GMT | By Dhwani Mehta

Bloomberg reports comments from the Swiss National Bank (SNB) Chairman Thomas Jordan delivered in an interview with NZZ.

Key Headlines:

Looking forward to others tightening monetary policy.

From a Swiss perspective, it would certainly be ideal if other jurisdictions would -- where possible -- normalize monetary policy.

Swiss system of separating banking & financial market supervision and monetary policy "reasonable".
