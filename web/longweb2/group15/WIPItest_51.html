F1JO Only Rocks With Liquor in “No Beer” Video

Christopher HunterPublished: March 29, 2017
  • SHARE
  • TWEET

Queens rapper and Eardruma affiliate F1JO is out to prove he's bringing the spotlight back to his borough. He recently dropped his club banger “No Beer” and today, he’s back with the visual for the gritty street hit.

Shot near the Jamaica, Queens housing projects, “No Beer” captures the street and gully side of the borough that so many musicians call home. F1JO is caught throughout the three-and-a-half minute video kicking it with his crew in front of the neighborhood deli and strolling through the projects. He drops a few bars in front of the well-known Queens Unisphere. He’s also reminding everyone that he’s not rocking with beer so don't bring it to his table in the club.

“Tell the waiter bring them bottles over here/I been trappin’, I been hustlin’ all year,” F1JO raps on the opening verse. “Rollie on my wrist, I’m a have the time/50 pointers on the bezel that just shine/Scoopa did a pair like a g/You know I'm on that green like Luigi/Word to my motherfucking momma/These niggas don't want no fuckin’ drama/I’m feeling militant like Osama nigga/Got them trapper sticks and them llamas nigga.”

F1JO dropped another gide a few weeks ago called “I’m From Queens.” The track's title is pretty much self-explanatory. The young MC hails from the borough that Nicki Minaj, Russell Simmons, 50 Cent, LL Cool J and Nas rep. The Queens native has also worked with the likes of Waka Flocka Flame, Gucci Mane, Rae Sremmurd and Dave East.
